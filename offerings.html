Clarity Uncorked

A weeklong retreat in Southwest France

Clarity has a way of showing up when you step out of your routine and into a place that feels both grounding and inspiring.

That's the idea behind Clarity Uncorked, a weeklong, all-inclusive retreat I co-created, set in Southwest France, that blends coaching with the kind of French food, wine, and cultural experiences that make you slow down and actually feel present again.

We stay in a private maison de maître, a stately old French home with high ceilings, bright rooms, and the kind of quiet elegance that instantly shifts your energy. It's not a resort. It's not a conference center. It's a home — filled with long dinners, market mornings, vineyard strolls, thoughtful conversation, and real space to breathe.

Throughout the week, I lead clarity-focused coaching sessions designed to help you reconnect with what you want next in your life and career. The setting does half the work; the coaching helps you make meaning out of it.

It's an intimate, curated experience for women who need perspective, connection, and a softer runway into reinvention.
